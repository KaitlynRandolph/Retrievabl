Our Mission

Here at Retrievabl, we want to give more transparency to news searching. We hope to give you a clearer view of the news you are consuming by giving you information about why our search results are ranked the way they are. We have based our search algorithm off the idea that overly-negative news has a large impact on how people think about key topics covered in news articles. We hope that by improving the visibility of less-negative news articles, we can make a positive impact on society.
